## Overview

**Author**: 宋鸿兵 (Song Hongbing)

The Currency Wars series explores the hidden world of international finance, central banking, and monetary policy throughout history. It examines how financial elites have shaped global events through control of money supply and banking systems.

### Book 1: 货币战争1：债务的魔咒 (Currency Wars 1: The Curse of Debt)
Explores the history of international banking families, particularly the Rothschilds, and their influence on world events.

### Book 2: 货币战争2：金权天下 (Currency Wars 2: The World of Financial Power)
Examines 300 years of financial power groups in Europe and America, their formation, conflicts, and cooperation.

### Book 3: 货币战争3：金融高边疆 (Currency Wars 3: Financial Frontier)
Analyzes financial history in China, including the Opium Wars, the relationship between financial and military power, and the evolution of China's monetary system.

### Book 4: 货币战争4：战国时代 (Currency Wars 4: The Warring States Era)
Discusses the modern era of currency competition, the rise of the Euro, and the strategic importance of reserve currencies in global politics.

### Book 5: 货币战争5：山雨欲来 (Currency Wars 5: The Storm is Coming)
Examines the 2013 gold market crash, the future of the US dollar, quantitative easing policies, and the looming global financial crisis. Topics include the "April 12th Gold Massacre," the battle between "Chinese aunties" and Wall Street, and the irreversible trap of QE policies.
